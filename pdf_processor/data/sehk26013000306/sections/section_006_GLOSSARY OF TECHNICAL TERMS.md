molding and curing

output remains constant and stable, with no perceptible rapid
variations in brightness or intensity that could cause visual
discomfort, eye strain, or neurological effects in sensitive
individuals

“MW” megawatt, equal to one million watts of electric power

“MWh” megawatt hour, a unit of electrical energy equal to one million

“new energy” various energy forms other than traditional energy, also known
as unconventional energy, such as solar energy, geothermal

“new energy vehicle dedicated charging equipment for new energy vehicles that

“oil-immersed a transformer using insulating oil as cooling and insulating

“Panama power an integrated HVDC power supply architecture for data centers,
supply” named after the “one-step” efficiency of the Panama Canal,

links such as traditional transformers, low-voltage distribution

transformation, rectification and DC distribution

“phase-shift a special transformer that adjusts the phase angle of the power

“power supply” a device that supplies a specific voltage, current (or power) to a

“PPS” polyphenylene sulfide, a high-performance thermoplastic

mechanical properties, which maintains stable physical and
chemical properties in high-temperature, corrosive and harsh
industrial environments

“pulse rectification” a rectification method that converts AC into DC by using pulse

of power electronic devices

“PV” photovoltaic, a technology that directly converts light energy

semiconductor interface

“Q40” a dedicated test condition identifier in reliability testing,
